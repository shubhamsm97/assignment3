Montserrat is a geometric sans-serif typeface designed by Argentine graphic designer
Julieta Ulanovsky and released in 2011. It was inspired by posters, signs and painted windows
from the first half of the twentieth century, seen in the historic Montserrat neighbourhood of
Buenos Aires. The project was started in 2010 by Ulanovsky and was released through the Google
Fonts catalogue in 2011
